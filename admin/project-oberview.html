Courier & Logistics System Architecture

Core System Capabilities

Shipment & History

  • Shipment booking, creation, and manifest generation.
  • Real-time tracking and status history logging.
  • Management of shipment items (content, weight, dimensions).
  • Automated assignment to drivers/riders.

Hub & Rider Management

  • Definition and management of multi-branch hubs.
  • Rider/driver profiles and assignment management.
  • Hub sorting and internal transfer logic.
  • Tracking of location data for status updates.

Rate & Invoicing

  • Pricing & rate tables (zone-based, weight slabs, volumetric).
  • Vendor/carrier pricing management for cost control.
  • Generation of invoices, daily statements, and payment records.
  • Integration of payment/order details.

APIs & Partners

  • Direct carrier API integrations (DHL, UPS, Aramex) for external bookings/tracking.
  • User portal for customer bookings and self-service tracking.

Key Database Entities

User & Access Control

  • users
  • roles
  • user_roles
  • branches
  • audits / activity_logs

Operational Assets

  • hubs (Physical locations)
  • carriers (External partners)
  • customers (Primary account holders)
  • assignments (Driver / Shipment linking)

Shipment Core

  • shipments
  • shipment_items (Contents/pieces)
  • shipment_status_log (History)
  • tracking_locations (Geographic updates)

Finance & Rates

  • rates
  • rate_zones
  • rate_slabs (Weight/volume structure)
  • orders_payments / invoices
